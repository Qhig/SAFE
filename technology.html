How It Works

From ground vibrations to actionable traffic insights

Vibration Detection
Seismic sensors buried alongside roads detect minute ground vibrations caused by vehicle movement, capturing signals as small as 0.1 nanometers.
1
Signal Processing
Advanced algorithms filter noise and extract vehicle signatures, analyzing frequency patterns, amplitude, and duration to identify vehicle characteristics.
2
AI Classification
Machine learning models trained on millions of vehicle signatures classify vehicle type, estimate weight, calculate speed, and determine direction of travel.
3
Data Fusion
Multiple sensor readings are combined to create comprehensive traffic patterns, eliminating false positives and enhancing accuracy through sensor redundancy.
4
Real-time Analytics
Cloud-based analytics engines process data streams to generate live traffic insights, predictive models, and automated alerts for traffic management systems.
