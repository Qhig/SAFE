Our Team

Core Team

Leading researchers and engineers transforming traffic monitoring through seismic innovation

FB
Florent Brenguier
Professor, Grenoble/ISTerre University
Florent has been leading the development of noise-based seismic monitoring technologies for over a decade. He is the recipient of the 2009 American Geophysical Union Aki Young Scientist Award. At SAFE, Florent leads the scientific development of our seismic detection algorithms and oversees the validation of our vehicle classification systems across different urban environments.
Seismic Monitoring Signal Processing Scientific Leadership
QH
Quentin Higueret
PhD Student, University Grenoble Alpes
Quentin specializes in large array seismic datasets and is an expert in continuous signal processing and machine learning techniques. At SAFE, he developed the core algorithms that power our real-time vehicle detection system, creating innovative solutions for processing massive streams of seismic data and implementing advanced ML models for traffic pattern recognition.
Machine Learning Signal Processing Algorithm Development
AM
Aurélien Mordret
Professor at GEUS, Geological Survey of Denmark and Greenland
Aurélien developed the first applications of noise-based passive seismic imaging using dense industrial seismic networks in offshore environments. His background as a Geophysics Engineer and experience in the Oil & Gas industry brings crucial expertise to SAFE's sensor deployment strategies and network optimization for urban traffic monitoring applications.
Seismic Imaging Network Design Industrial Applications
